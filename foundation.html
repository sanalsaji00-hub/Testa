Foundation Programs for Classes 6-10

Program Description

The Foundation Programs at TESTA Academy are designed for students in Classes 6 to 10 to build a strong conceptual understanding of Mathematics and Science. These programs lays a solid academic foundation for future competitive exams such as NEET, JEE, and KEAM.

Conducted mainly on weekends and holidays, the Foundation Coaching Programs supports the school syllabus while developing logical thinking, problem-solving skills, and exam confidence.
